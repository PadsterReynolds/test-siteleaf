Currencies Card

First of its kind in Saudi Arabia is the Currencies Card which is a visa debit card offered free of charge and without annuals fees. The card can be linked to 4 different currencies designed for your convenience and saving when travelling abroad.

It's free!

Issued free of charge, and no annual fees

Charge free POS transactions

Absolutely no fees on point of sale transactions

Total Control

Depending on your needs, you may link and unlink foreign accounts to your currencies card anytime anywhere through meem's app or internet banking

Apply Now!

Additional Currency Card Benefits

Instant Issaunce!

Apply for the currencies card and get it immediately. Collect it in store on the spot or by courier in 3 business days

Low fees on ATM withdrawals

Only 2.25 % is charged when withdrawing cash from an ATM

Variety of currencies

BHD, AED, QAR, OMR, KWD, EUR, GBP, and USD are the currencies available to be linked to the card

Base currency feature

Select a base currency to convert into other currencies if the currency of your transaction isn't available 

Fast Exchange and Transfer Anytime, Anywhere

Currency Exchange Calculator

To avoid any surprises in the exchange rates when transferring funds to your foreign currency accounts, use the Currency Exchange Calculator to check the rates in real time.

Moving funds

To transfer funds into your foreign currency accounts select the icon on top right corner “Move Money” and select accounts you want to transfer funds from and to. Also, you will be able to see the current currency exchange rate before making the transfer.
