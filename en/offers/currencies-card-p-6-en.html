How To Apply?

New Customers

First you will need to open a meem One Pack Account to be able to apply for meem's products. Opening the One Pack Account can be done simply by following these steps:
 

  1. Click on the "Apply Now!" button below
  2. Fill in the application form
  3. Visit the nearest meem main store to complete your account opening. 
  4. Once you have your account set up, you may follow the steps of how to apply for existing meemers.

Existing Customers

  1. Login to your account
  2. Click on the products icon
  3. Select Murabaha Deposit from the list
  4. Press on "Add New Product"
  5. Follow the application instruction and you will be able to place your Murabaha Deposit within 5 minutes!
