Contact us

At meem, it's all about you. Therefore, we make sure you can reach us wherever, whenever and for whatever reason.

So where do you turn when you want to talk to someone here at meem?

In meem app or meem online:

Follow these steps to send us your enquiries, feedback, complaints:

  1. Log in to your meem account
  2. Click on “Talk to meem”
  3. Click on “Create new”
  4. Select your message type and subject. Then write your message.
  5. Click on “Send”!
